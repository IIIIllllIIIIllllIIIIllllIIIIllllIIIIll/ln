A vector subspace is a subset of a vector space that is also a vector space.

\section{Sum}

Suppose $U_1, ... U_m$ are subspaces of $V$; then 

\begin{align}
U_1 + ... U_m = \{u_1 + ... u_m | u_i \in U_i\}
\end{align}

is the sum of all possible sums of elements of $U$.
